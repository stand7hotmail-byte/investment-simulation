# Asset Class Reference

Commonly used asset classes and their typical ticker symbols (Yahoo Finance format).

## Stock (株式)
- **US:** VTI (Total Stock), VOO (S&P 500), QQQ (Nasdaq 100), VYM (High Dividend)
- **Japan:** 1306.T (TOPIX), 1321.T (Nikkei 225), 2558.T (S&P 500 JPY), 1557.T (S&P 500)

## Bond (債券)
- **US:** BND (Total Bond), AGG (Aggregate), TLT (20+ Yr Treasury)
- **Japan:** 2510.T (Domestic Govt), 2511.T (Foreign Govt)

## Commodity (コモディティ)
- GLD (Gold), SLV (Silver), DBC (Commodity Index), USO (Oil)

## REIT (不動産)
- VNQ (US Real Estate), IYR (US Real Estate), 1343.T (TSE REIT), 1555.T (Australian REIT)

## Crypto (暗号資産)
- BTC-USD (Bitcoin), ETH-USD (Ethereum), SOL-USD (Solana)
